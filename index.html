How to Use Flashcard Creator

Step 1: Add Flashcards Manually

To add flashcards manually, use the "Add Flashcard" button. This will create a new flashcard with fields for the front and back text. Enter your question in the "Front" field and the answer in the "Back" field.

Step 2: Delete Flashcards

If you need to remove a flashcard, click the "Delete" button next to the flashcard you want to delete. This will remove the flashcard from the list.

Step 3: Upload Flashcards from CSV

You can also upload flashcards from a CSV file. The file should have two columns: one for questions (Front) and one for answers (Back). Click the "Upload CSV" button and select your file to upload flashcards in bulk.

Step 4: Generate PDF

Once you have added all your flashcards, click the "Generate PDF" button. This will create a PDF document with your flashcards formatted for printing. The PDF will have fronts and backs intercalated for easy double-sided printing.

Example CSV Format

Your CSV file should be structured as follows:

Front,Back
Question 1,Answer 1
Question 2,Answer 2
...
